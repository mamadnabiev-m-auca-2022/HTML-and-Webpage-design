What is the purpose of the landing page (your site)?
The purpose of the landing page is to promote a specific product or service, in this case, high-quality laptops. It aims to inform visitors about the available laptop options, including high-performance laptops and sleek and lightweight ones.

Who is your target audience?
The target audience includes professionals in need of powerful laptops for work, students looking for affordable and reliable laptops for their studies, and tech enthusiasts seeking high-quality laptops that cater to their specific requirements. These users may have diverse demographics and interests but share an interest in laptops.

What is the call to action (CTA)?
The primary call to action (CTA) on the landing page is "Buy Now." This CTA guides visitors towards the desired action of purchasing a laptop. Additionally, there's a "Categories" button to provide users with options for exploring different laptop categories.
